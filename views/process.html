This is why I chose to be a designer

My design process was inspired by my pursuit of filmmaking and storytelling. When I started filmmaking in 2016, I found value in the collection of visual memories I was able to share with friends and family. Furthermore, I found post-production editing to be a fascinating process where I could combine and augment variables of audio and visual design to tell a certain story, and possibly inspire another person to travel more or appreciate the value of filmmaking.

Similar to filmmaking, design allows me to be critical of every decision I make, all of the nuances that must be considered before even evaluating the visual design. I believe there exists an inherent beauty in the marriage of different principles of structure and design, consequently its effects on a user's experience.
